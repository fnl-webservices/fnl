Procurement and Supply of Materials and Equipment for Oil and Gas Industries

Oil & Gas Industry Equipment

We provide an equipment and material procurement and supply service to the oil and gas industry throughout Nigeria . Procurement in the Oil & Gas Industry has become an essential service in today’s competitive marketplace. Our role is to find and supply all the OEM manufactured materials and spare parts associated with the production and delivery of oil and gas as well as its maintenances. Our clients expect an exceptional level of service and we fulfil that expectation by delivering a reliable, OEM standard, diligent and conscientious service that meets their specific demands. With many years experience in the oil and gas regional services. Fedoz Nigeria Limited has relationships with renowned OEM manufacturers, major pumps and spares suppliers in the world, across Europe, USA, Far East and the Sub-Continent and are able to supply equipment to the correct specification, on time and within budget. Working for oil and gas companies primarily in Nigeria we can procure and ship all equipment and material used in production, asset management , fire-fighting, oil spill recovery, instrumentation, Anti-theft protection, marine services and gas product related services.
