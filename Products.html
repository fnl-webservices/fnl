Adittionaly, we offer pump reverse engineering, Reverse engineering is a process by which the design of an equipment, object or system is re created through the analysis of its structure, function and operation using a physical part as a starting point.
Our Ruhrpumpen's Aftermarket services can replicate broken or worn pump parts for any pump model from any manufacturer. This way, we develop the spare parts of discontinued pump models so our clients don’t have to invest in a brand new pump just because spare parts
are no longer available. The reverse engineering process needs hardware and software that work together. Our unique team of highly trained engineers and modeling experts use the latest technology in solid modeling software and coordinate measuring machines. Equipment and worn parts are measured using advanced 3 D scanning technologies. The generated 3D model is then used to develop manufacturing drawings for the new part. This process also provides the opportunity to add value and enhance or modify the original equipment specifications to meet or exceed its current performance.

A cost effective solution to restore a damaged or worn part.Hundreds of industrial operations are using pumps that have been in service for more than 30 years. We offer on site reverse engineering services during maintenance outages so, if needed, the part never leaves the plant and production cycles are unaffected. As we manage the entire manufacturing process of the new part, we are able to provide our clients with replacement parts at competitive prices with shorter delivery times.

ANTI- THEFT SECURITY LOCKS!

Fedoz Nigeria Limited is the nation's leading Supplier of high-end anti-theft devices. We are OEM Representative for Mcgard , We work in close partnership with our customers to develop innovative solutions. Pipeline companies face growing need in their assets protection from different kinds of malicious activities. Most of those are related to fuel theft and pipeline vandalism.There are few traditional solutions for pipeline security which are based on acoustic, pressure, flow rate and other statistic methods of protection. Fedoz is the nation’s leading supplier of mechanical anti-theft devices. The evolution of our unique Intimidating line of products has since provided innovative security solutions for access / perimeter control, theft / removal prevention for Oil and Gas Installations in Remote areas, public utilities, Flow stations of oil fields, high security facilities equipment, and many more.

Plug Lock: 
The Intimidator Plug Lock protects against liquid or gas losses. In seconds, the Plug Lock threads into any female-threaded connection where it fits slightly recessed, with no protruding parts to tempt unauthorized removal. The Plug Lock has no moving parts to jam, freeze or be picked. Constructed of through-hardened, high-quality alloy steel, the Plug Lock can be installed or removed with a McGard key (tool). Available in a variety of diameters.

Flange Lock:
Avoid lost production, valve replacement and wellhead workover with the Intimidator Flange Lock. This uniquely designed lock, based on advanced McGard technology, is drastically reducing oil field theft of x-mas trees and valves. Simply replace two opposing bolts or studs at each flanged connection with The Intimidator Flange Lock. Flange Locks are made of high-strength alloy steel, selectively hardened for strength and durability.

Pipe Lock:
Now you can secure pipe shut-offs reliably and quickly with The Intimidator Pipe Lock. The Pipe Lock prevents unauthorized siphoning of oil and gas from wells, remote storage tanks, and other containment areas. It is also adaptable for use with water storage tanks. Each Pipe Lock is constructed of a heavy-duty outer shell that spins freely around an inner cap. This cap is threaded over the pipe that is being protected or shut off. A retaining ring holds the inner cap into the outer shell. Milled into the face of the inner cap is a computer designed, narrow groove pattern. The Pipe Lock requires a matching key (tool) for installation and removal.

Barton Meter Lock: 
you can protect Barton or similar type meters from oil field theft. The Intimidator Barton Meter Lock is a total security system that includes a security bolt, security locks and a cable lock. Based on advanced McGard technology, it provides a complete tamper-resistant system for the protection of oil field meters.

McGard’s Intimidator line of security products is a family of security locks that are superior to any other mechanical theft devices on the market. The McGard locking pattern technology is unique in the industry. Installation or removal of each lock is accomplished with its matching key (tool). McGard offers computer designed extra-narrow groove patterns that can be cost-effectively adapted to an unlimited range of applications. Key registration, to authorized personnel, and computerized tracking of key codes assures you that no unauthorized keys will be distributed in your geographic region. Every Intimidator product is made of only the highest grades of alloy steel and heat treated for maximum strength. We offer a complete range of plating types to match your requirements and industry accepted plating specifications.
