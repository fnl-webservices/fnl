About Fedoz Nigeria Limited

We have the knowledge and years of experience in Oil and Gas Services

Fedoz Nigeria Limited is a wholly an indigenous Oil and Gas Servicing Company. Incorporated with the Company and Allied Corporation(CAC) in the year 2002 with RC NO: 459607. we have developed local content development strategy in accordance with the provision of National Local Content Development Plan, and also with regulatory permits to operate in this sector.

Who We Are

FEDOZ NIGERIA LIMITED is an Oil & Gas Services, headquartered in Port-Harcourt, Nigeria. FEDOZ is the provider of Highest Quality Oil and Gas equipment and services, widely recognized in the region for setting a standard within the Oil and Gas industry. The company’s clients include Onshore and Offshore Oil and Gas industries all over West Africa and beyond.

Our Vision

we exist to; materialize projects requiring advanced technology and specialization, create benefits for our employees, our company and end users, provide the satisfaction of our customers in maximum level in design, engineering, procuremnt & supply, construction, testing and commisioning jobs of pipeline, pipeline, oil & gas facility and Infracstructure construction projects in energy sector of various industries.

Founded in 2002, the company was established with the focus of providing specialized services to the Oil and Gas industries, especially for the Pumps technology . Highly specialized, and equipped with an expert team committed to innovation and productivity, the company established a strong foothold in the field of Pumps Maintenance & Installation, Anti-Theft protection system , Fire control systems, Asset Mangament. What sets us apart is our outstanding track records with our clients, as well as our prompt after-sales support.
